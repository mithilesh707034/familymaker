Relationship Workshops Santulan-Making Gender Differences Work

The objective of the Santulan workshop is to help men and women understand each other better so that they can live and work in harmony with each other.

The workshop includes-
  • Compare the differences between Men and Women.
  • Explore the reason behind the differences between men and women.
  • Implication of the differences in our day-to-day life.
  • Solutions to bridge the Gap.

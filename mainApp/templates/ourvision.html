Vision

To be a globally acclaimed thought leader helping create happy Homes.
Mission of defining various aspects of a HOME. Multiple dimensions work simultaneously in a home like gender differences, leadership, relationships, parenting etc. Relating each aspect at Home to Workplace (Skill building, Leadership, team work etc.) to include Home in the productive category.

Mona believes in holistic life which leads to comprehensive individual development including all facets of living like physical, emotional, intellectual, social and spiritual. Mona found that Home is the center of our living, yet it is not included in the learning arena. She does trainings to include home into the learning area for holistic development and for better work-home balance.

Multiple aspects work simultaneously in a home like chores, parenting, relationships, couple differences and similarities etc. She defines various aspects to show the origin of various skills from a Home to include work in a Home in productive category.

Mona is an experienced risk management professional who has worked with Indian and multinational firms and have 15+ years of experience assisting corporates in future planning and profit maximization. She ventured into Professional Speaking and Trainings in 2015 when she started undertaking programs on various facets of a Home.
